About Us

The sophomore scripps company is a non profit organisation formed by a group of officers from public sector enterprises to create a platform for recognising and celebrating young talents across the country.

In pursuit of our vision, we aim to cultivate a vibrant and inclusive learning community, where educators are passionate and dedicated mentors, fostering intellectual curiosity, critical thinking, and creativity. We seek to forge meaningful partnerships with schools, institutions, and organizations to drive positive change in education on a global scale
