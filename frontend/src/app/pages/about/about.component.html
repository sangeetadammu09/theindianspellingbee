Why Choose Us!

The sophomore scripps company is a non profit organisation formed by a group of officers from public sector enterprises to create a platform for recognising and celebrating young talents across the country.

Our vision
To empower and inspire learners of all ages to reach their full potential and become lifelong learners who positively impact their communities and the world.We envision a world where barriers to education are overcome, and learners from diverse backgrounds have equal opportunities to achieve academic success and personal growth.In pursuit of our vision, we aim to cultivate a vibrant and inclusive learning community, where educators are passionate and dedicated mentors, fostering intellectual curiosity, critical thinking, and creativity. We seek to forge meaningful partnerships with schools, institutions, and organizations to drive positive change in education on a global scale
Our mission
The Sophomore scripps great Indian spelling bee primary objective is to foster healthy competition among the students and to challenge themselves in this ever changing competitive world.our spelling bee competition recognize and celebrate academic achievement in the realm of language arts,paves a great way for community involvement to students, teachers and parents.Excelling in spelling bee can be an impressive addition to a student's resume or college application. It showcases a commitment to academic excellence and dedication to personal growth
